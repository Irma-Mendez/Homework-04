My favourite dessert: Orange cake

This is a very traditional orange cake, and keeps some similarities with the well known Pound cake but with an extra tasty flavour of orange. This recipe has been passed down for generations since my grandmother, and it is the default type of cake that the complete family eats without a problem for birthdays or any special occasion, is relatively simple to make and quite delicious!

By: Irma Roxana Mendez Cantero

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of Flour
  • 750 grams of unsalted butter
  • 3/4 kilo of eggs
  • 3 and a half cups of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla escence
  • 1/2 liter of orange juice
  • 1/2 cup of milk (or more if needed)
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of baking soda

Procedure

  1. Take the butter out of the fridge to make sure it is soft before mixing it.
  2. Mix the butter with all the sugar until the sugar grains are not palpable. 😊
  3. While you do that sift the flour and mix it with baking powder and baking soda
  4. Add that to the mixture of butter and sugar
  5. Also, separate the whites from the yolks of all the eggs, and reserve the whites.
  6. Add the yolks to the main mixture
  7. Now you can add orange juice gradually 🍊
  8. At this moment you can add the vanilla escence too
  9. Using the egg whites that were reserved, whisk them until they form stiff and white peaks
  10. Add them to the main mixture gently so that they don't lose air, folding the egg whites genlty into the batter.
  11. If the mixture is still too thick, you can add some milk to get to the right consistency of the batter *must be no too liquid but no too thick 😉
  12. Take out your bakeware and grease them with a thin layer of butter and flour
  • You can also use wax paper to cover the insides of your cake molds
  • Fill half the height of the molds with the batter (so that they don't spill inside the oven).
  • Put the molds inside the oven at 180Â°C for an hour. 🔥
  • Before taking them out:
    • While the cake is still inside the oven insert a toothpick or a thin knife into it, if it comes out clean it means the cake is already cooked and ready to come out. 🥧
  • Take the molds out and wait 20 minutes or until the cool down
  • After that you can take the cakes out of their molds and decorate them, or straight up eat a slice or two 🍰
  • Enjoy! 🥳
